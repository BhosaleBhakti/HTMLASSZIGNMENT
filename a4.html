SELENA GOMEZ


      Selena Gomez is an American singer, actress, and producer who has become one of the most influential figures in modern entertainment. Born on July 22, 1992, in Grand Prairie, Texas, she began her career as a child actress on Barney & Friends before rising to fame with her starring role in Disney Channel’s Wizards of Waverly Place. Alongside acting, Selena pursued music and initially formed the band Selena Gomez & the Scene, releasing hits like “Naturally.” She later transitioned into a solo career, with albums such as Stars Dance and Revival showcasing her growth as an artist. Songs like “Come & Get It,” “Good for You,” and “Hands to Myself” cemented her place in pop music, admired for her emotional and heartfelt vocal style. Beyond music, Selena has produced projects like the Netflix series 13 Reasons Why and has been deeply involved in philanthropy, becoming the youngest UNICEF ambassador at age 17. She is also recognized as a fashion icon
